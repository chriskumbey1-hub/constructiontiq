About ConstructIQ

ConstructIQ is a comprehensive digital platform built specifically for the Ghanaian property development and construction industry. Whether you are a first-time homeowner building your dream bungalow in Kumasi, a developer managing multiple residential projects in East Legon, or a commercial investor in Tema — ConstructIQ gives you the professional tools to plan, budget, track, and execute with confidence. Every feature is calibrated to Ghana's climate, construction standards, and market rates.
